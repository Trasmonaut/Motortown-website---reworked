Our Story

"Hello, I'm MrHwang from South Korea. I love driving games so much that I started to make my own game back in 2002 but failed to finish it. So I joined a game company and worked there for 14 years to learn how to make a game. And in summer 2020, I finally quit the job and start to make my dream game again." - Mr Hwang

MotorTown: Behind The Wheel is a passion project by P3 Games, a small indie game studio based in South Korea. Founded by MrHwang, the team is dedicated to creating an immersive driving experience that combines realistic physics with a vibrant open world.

With a focus on community feedback and continuous improvement, P3 Games aims to deliver a game that not only meets but exceeds player expectations.
