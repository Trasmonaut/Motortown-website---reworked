Frequently Asked Questions

General

Console/Mobile/VR?

Currently, we are focused on the PC version of the game. We have no plans for console, mobile, or VR versions at this time.

What is different between Demo and Full version?

Demo has a level 2 limitation and you cannot buy a house.Full list of vehicles you can own in Demo: trophy taxi, pickup, stella, ranch pickup, stinger, van, bora.You can also drive Police interceptor and Small bus.

Can I keep the progress from Demo if I buy the full version?

Yes, you can keep playing your character from Demo.

Note: you cannot load characters with more than level 2 in Demo, meaning once you play in Early Access, you cannot load that character in Demo.

Can demo version players connect with full version players?

No, Motor Town uses Steam's matchmaking system, which does not support cross-play between Demo and Full versions.

I lost my save game, how can I get it back?

Email MrHwang with your save files, a description of the problem, and your character name: p3games.motortown@gmail.com

Controllers are supported?

Yes. Xbox controllers, Steering Wheels, and Joysticks are supported. NOTE: Some UI elements still require a mouse.

My PS4 Controller is not working

Check 'PlayStation Configuration Support' in Steam's Controller settings.More info in the following link: here

Why can't I get my controller/wheel to work?

  • For controllers: Ensure you've selected "controller" in control settings and that your controller is PC-compatible.
  • For wheels: Click on the wheel category in control settings to select the wheel.
  • If using a Thrustmaster wheel, install the Thrustmaster Control Panel.
  • If using a PS3/PS4 button/switch, use PS3 mode when playing on PC.

Bug reposts can be made on the offical Discrod Sever: here

How can I bind Combined Axis Pedal?

While binding Axis, set Min 0% - Max 50% to one pedal and Min 50% - Max 100% to the other. Use 'Invert' if needed.

Will there be Steam Mod/Workshop support?

Probably, but for now, we are focused on adding more content to the game.

What engine is this game made with?

Unreal Engine 5.

How to fix game starting in VR mode?

Right-click Motor Town in your Steam library, go to properties, and uncheck "Use Desktop Game Theatre while SteamVR is active"

Gameplay / Game Content

How can I pause the game?

You need to turn off 'Allow' when you start the game. Then press 'ESC' to stop the game

Where can I find X car?

Cars spawn in specific locations. You can find a list of car locations on the Unofficial Wiki: here

Does the weight of the passengers (friends multi) count for the performances?

Yes, the weight of passengers affects vehicle performance.Seat location also affects weight balance.

How can I remove items from the quick slots?

Press 'Tab' and drag & drop items to the ground.

Is the island map based on anything?

Yes, it is based on Jeju Island in South Korea.

Why is the distance under the map different from what we see on screen?

The distance under the map represents the road distance to the destination, while the large on-screen icon shows the direct path.

How do you store (despawn) cars?

Visit a garage or a parking space large enough to fit your car.

How do I get my car back after despawning?

You can retrieve your car from the garage or parking space by interacting with it.

How do I stop renting a car?

Simply get out of the vehicle. You are only charged while in the vehicle.

How can I hide the HUD?

Press ALT + Z. You can change this keybind in the settings if needed.

How do I disable Automatic shifting?

Press ALT + A

What is that recharge icon next to fuel in Zydro?

It's the regenerative braking level. You can change it using the gear up or down buttons The stroger the regenerative braking, the more energy you can recover when braking.

How do I view my character stats?

On the main menu, click the "Character" button.

Why does the Tow Truck have a siren?

It's a Korean thing! Tow trucks in Korea often have loud sirens and flashy lights
